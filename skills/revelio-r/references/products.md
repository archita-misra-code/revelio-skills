# Revelio Products and WRDS Notes

Use this reference to choose the right Revelio WRDS product/table before querying.

## General

- Revelio is a monthly-updated workforce database built from public professional profiles, job postings, and employee sentiment reviews.
- WRDS does not retain prior monthly snapshots for most products.
- Revelio Labs does not have an official LinkedIn partnership; individual data availability is limited to public portions of profiles visible without logging in at collection time.
- Deleted/private profiles can remain in Revelio if they were once public. Use `individual_user.updated_dt` as a freshness diagnostic; records more than roughly three months stale are more likely to be profiles that are no longer publicly scrapeable.
- Coverage is global but depends on user-generated source availability.
- Standardized fields are much smaller and easier to query than raw text fields.
- Some raw fields, especially job descriptions, are available only on WRDS Postgres.
- IDs such as `position_id` can be negative because they are generated by hashing.
- Not all positions or job postings have an associated company/RCID.

## Common

`revelio_common.company_mapping`

- Maps `rcid` to company names, common identifiers, subsidiaries, ultimate parent companies, headquarters geography, and RICS industry categories.
- Useful identifiers include `isin`, `cusip`, `cik`, `sedol`, `ticker`, `gvkey`, and `lei`.
- Use `ultimate_parent_rcid` for parent-level aggregation.
- Subsidiary treatment may be pro-forma rather than point-in-time.

Other common tables include `school_mapping`, `regions`, and `months`.

## Individual

Coverage starts in 2008 and is based on public LinkedIn profiles.

Tables:

- `individual_user`: profile information, including predicted gender/ethnicity, prestige, highest degree, profile URL, user country, and refresh date.
- `individual_positions`: employment history, role clusters, geography, seniority, predicted salary, RCID, company, and ultimate parent company.
- `individual_user_education`: education history, mapped school, degree, field, and school country.
- `individual_user_skills`: reported and predicted skills.
- Raw text tables include `individual_user_raw`, `individual_positions_raw`, and `individual_user_education_raw`.
- `individual_role_lookup_v3`: role clusters and O*NET mappings.
- `individual_user_skill_lookup`: skill taxonomy lookup.

Prediction caveat: gender, ethnicity, salary, standardized roles, skills, and sentiment scores are Revelio-created values, not directly reported measures.

Date caveat: tables without dates, such as Individual Skills, should generally be interpreted as current values only. `first_reported` can support limited timing checks for skills.

## Skills Taxonomy

April 2026 update:

- Revelio expanded from roughly 3,000 to more than 30,000 distinct skills.
- `skill_mapped` was replaced by `skill_k35000`.
- Join `individual_user_skills` to `individual_user_skill_lookup` using `skill_k35000`.
- Use broader categories up to `skill_k15` for aggregated analysis.

## Job Postings

`revelio_job_postings.postings_cosmos`

- COSMOS postings are sourced from company websites, major job boards, staffing firms, and aggregators, then deduplicated.
- Coverage starts in 2020, with some earlier coverage.
- Key fields include `job_id`, `company`, `country`, `role_k1500_v2`, `role_k17000_v3`, `salary`, `salary_min`, `salary_max`, `salary_predicted`, `post_date`, `remove_date`, `rcid`, `ultimate_parent_rcid`, `remote_type`, `expected_hires`, and source flags.
- Some postings have no RCID.
- Raw postings text is in `postings_cosmos_raw`, joined by `job_id`, and can be very large.
- `postings_cosmos_raw` is Postgres-only and includes `job_id`, `description`, `title_raw`, `jobtitle_translated`, and `location_raw`.
- Use `postings_role_lookup_v3` for COSMOS v3 role categories, including `role_k17000_v3`, `role_k1500_v3`, `role_k150_v3`, `role_k50_v3`, `role_k10_v3`, and O*NET fields.
- WRDS moved from separate LinkedIn and aggregator postings feeds to consolidated COSMOS in June 2025. To approximate old feeds, filter COSMOS with `source_linkedin = 1` for LinkedIn-sourced postings, or `source_regional_aggregators = 1 OR source_other_aggregators = 1` for aggregator-sourced postings.

## Sentiment

Coverage starts in 2009 and is sourced from Glassdoor.

Tables:

- `sentiment_individual_reviews`: individual reviews with ratings, raw text fields, role, seniority, geography, and review dates.
- `sentiment_scores`: aggregate company-level sentiment scores by topic with `num_reviews`.

Only select raw text fields such as `review_summary`, `review_advice`, `review_pros`, and `review_cons` when doing text analysis.

SAS size limits can cause a tiny share of raw review text records to be missing from SAS-backed individual review tables; use Postgres for raw text work when possible.

## Workforce Dynamics

`revelio_workforce_dynamics.workforce_dynamics_geo`

- Monthly company composition for large global companies, starting in 2007.
- Segmented by geography, seniority, and `role_k10`.
- Includes headcount, inflow, outflow, external inflow/outflow, raw and scaled variants, salary, tenure duration, and remote suitability.

## Layoffs

`revelio_layoffs.layoffs`

- WARN notices connected to Revelio company identifiers.
- Mostly useful for US-focused analysis.
- Key fields include company, RCID, state/city, notice and layoff dates, number of employees, and layoff type fields.
